Perfect Tofu

Description

A perfect tofu dish balances crispiness, flavor, and texture, turning this humble soy-based protein into something truly satisfying. One standout example is crispy tofu broccoli stir-fry

The tofu is first pressed to remove excess moisture, then coated lightly with cornflour, salt, and pepper before being pan-fried until golden and crunchy. This ensures the tofu holds its shape and develops a delicious crust

Ingridients

  • Firm or extra-firm tofu
  • Cornflour or cornstarch
  • Soya sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Broccolio florets

    • Steps

    1. Press the tofu
    2. Cut and coat
    3. Pan-fry tofu
    4. Prepare vegetables
    5. Make the sauce
    6. combine and Finish the serve
